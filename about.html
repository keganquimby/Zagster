Our story

Ah, Paris in the spring.

Every cool business idea seems to start with a trip to somewhere exotic or an “a-ha!” moment while sitting at a café. We’re no different. The Zagster journey started with our use of the Paris-based bike-sharing program Vélib’. As young, urban bikers looking to make our mark on transportation, we had been following it from afar since it was first announced. After all, we always loved the concept of sharing things like cars and music, so why not bikes too?

But it wasn’t until we took our first trip to Paris and biked for hours that we were sold on the idea. We, Tim and Jason, wanted desperately to bring bike sharing to Philadelphia, our hometown. We wanted to make the cheesesteak city synonymous with bike sharing. Thus CityRyde — since renamed Zagster — was born. Now it’s five years later and we’re thinking a tad bigger — something between world domination and best-in-class turnkey bike sharing and rentals.

Street Cred

Over the last few years, Zagster has become an influential name in the bike-sharing and -rental movement. We’ve been consulting on (and implementing) bike-sharing systems and the environmental impact of bike use since 2007. Our years of experience and technical expertise allow us to assist our customers with every stage of their bike initiatives, from conception to implementation. More importantly, we’re also known for something pretty cool. Zagster is the only organization in the world that has created a carbon methodology, approved by The Carbon Powers That Be, that can accurately measure the carbon reductions associated with all types of bike use. The World Bank liked it so much, they’re partnering with us to roll it out globally. For us, it’s all about carbon — and we don’t mean diamonds. Plant trees. Use bikes. It’s that simple.

About the team.

We’re passionate cyclists who love fresh air, good exercise and emission-free transportation. We believe that we’re creating a better alternative to burning fossil fuels by providing easy-to-use bike-sharing and -rental systems. We want to use our years of experience to work toward the day when biking is a leading part of the transportation mix for people of all incomes, regardless of where you live, work or play. Zagster is not just a bike vendor; we’re a partner, and we’ll work with you to understand your goals and maximize your program’s performance every step of the way. Thanks to our years of experience and expertise in the bike-sharing industry, we know how to implement bike shares quickly and seamlessly, and we’re dedicated to bringing easily accessible bikes to the people.
